Frequently Asked Questions

At Pawsitively Yours, we offer various career opportunities for individuals passionate about animal welfare. From veterinary positions to administrative roles, we seek dedicated professionals who share our commitment to animal care and advocacy. Whether you're interested in animal behavior, shelter management, or veterinary medicine, we have rewarding career paths that align with your interests and skills.


At Pawsitively Yours, the well-being and care of animals are our top priorities. We provide a nurturing environment where animals receive love, attention, and medical care from our dedicated staff and volunteers. From regular veterinary check-ups to enrichment activities, we ensure that each animal's physical and emotional needs are met. Additionally, we offer adoption services to find loving forever homes for our furry friends, ensuring they receive the care and attention they deserve throughout their lives.


Pawsitively Yours understands the importance of proper nutrition for the health and well-being of animals. We provide balanced diets tailored to each animal's age, breed, and dietary needs. Our trained staff and volunteers ensure that animals receive nutritious meals and dietary supplements as needed. Additionally, we offer guidance and support to adopters on maintaining a healthy diet for their new pet, promoting lifelong wellness and vitality.


Pawsitively Yours offers a variety of volunteer opportunities for individuals passionate about making a difference in the lives of animals. From assisting with animal care and socialization to participating in adoption events and fundraising activities, there are numerous ways to get involved and support our mission. Whether you're a student, professional, or retiree, we welcome volunteers of all ages and backgrounds to join us in our efforts to help animals in need.


Pawsitively Yours offers a diverse selection of animals for adoption, including dogs, cats, rabbits, and small mammals. Each animal in our care undergoes thorough health and behavior assessments to ensure they are ready for their forever home. Whether you're looking for a playful puppy, a cuddly kitten, or a devoted companion, we have animals of all ages, breeds, and personalities waiting to meet their perfect match.


The adoption costs and fees at Pawsitively Yours vary depending on the type of animal, age, and any additional services provided. Our adoption fees typically cover spaying/neutering, vaccinations, microchipping, and a wellness exam by a licensed veterinarian. We strive to keep our adoption fees affordable while ensuring that each animal receives the necessary care and support to thrive in their new home.


Pawsitively Yours provides a range of veterinary services to promote the health and well-being of animals in our care. Our services include wellness exams, vaccinations, spaying/neutering, dental care, and treatment for common medical conditions. We work closely with licensed veterinarians to ensure that each animal receives high-quality medical care tailored to their individual needs.

We look forward to welcoming you to our facility and sharing more about our mission and the animals we serve.
